FAQ

How can I book a workspace?

Booking a workspace is quick and easy. Simply visit the BOOK section and follow these four simple steps:

  • Step 1: Search for an available workspace.
  • Step 2: Enter your persanl details and select a free time slot
  • Step 3: Save your reservation in your calendar and happy working!

How can I view my reservation?

To view your reservation, head over to the BOOK section and navigate to MANAGE RESERVATIONS. There, you will be able to find all your bookings starting from today and extending up to a year ahead.

How can I cancel my reservation?

Life can sometimes throw unexpected events on our way. If you need to cancel your reservation, don't worry! Just simply go to MANAGE RESERVATIONS and delete your booking. We will refund the entire amount you paid within 3 working days after cancellation.

How can I view my payments?

Want to double-check your payment details? No problem, we provide transparent invoices with a breakdown of all costs. You can find the invoice under MANAGE RESERVATIONS immediately after booking your workspace.

How can I import my reservation into my calendar?

To import your reservation into your preferred calendar, simply download the .ics file during the booking confirmation process or from the MANAGE RESERVATIONS section.

Couldn't find your answer here?

Our friendly support desk is here to assist you further. Please don't hesitate to reach out to us via email or phone. For additional information, please refer to our CONTACT page.
